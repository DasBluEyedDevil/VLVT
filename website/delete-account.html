Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. This page explains how to delete your VLVT account and request removal of your personal data.

The fastest way to delete your account is directly in the VLVT app:

4

Scroll down and tap "Delete Account," then confirm your decision

What Data Will Be Deleted?

The following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Account information (email, authentication data)
  • Profile information (name, age, bio, interests)
  • All photos you uploaded
  • All matches and conversations
  • Location data
  • App usage history
  • Device information

Data that may be retained:

  • Reports you made about other users (for safety purposes, anonymized)
  • Transaction records (required for legal/tax purposes, up to 7 years)
  • Data required to comply with legal obligations

Processing Time

  • In-app deletion: Immediate (profile removed within minutes)
  • Email/Form requests: Processed within 7 business days
  • Complete data erasure: Up to 30 days from request
  • Backup removal: Up to 90 days (backups are automatically overwritten)

Cancel Your Subscription First

If you have an active subscription, deleting your account does not automatically cancel it. To avoid future charges:
